EISCore uses a dual-track licensing model:

- Open source license: AGPL-3.0-or-later.
- Commercial license: separate written authorization from the copyright holder.

You may use, modify, distribute, and operate EISCore under the terms of
AGPL-3.0-or-later, including the source code availability obligations
that apply to network service use.

Any use that does not fully comply with AGPL-3.0-or-later requires
separate written authorization from the copyright holder, including:

- Closed-source commercial delivery.
- Government digitalization product declaration or project submission.
- Product ownership declaration or proprietary product integration.
- Software copyright registration based on EISCore or derivative works.
- SaaS operation, private deployment, or customer delivery without
  satisfying the AGPL source code disclosure obligations.

Open source publication does not transfer copyright, intellectual
property ownership, commissioned-development result ownership, buyout
rights, or exclusive commercial rights.
